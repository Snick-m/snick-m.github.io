Welcome To A new era of Digitalization
Finally the long awaited digitalization of Education is at our hand with the easiest possible technology that will bring a great change upon our loving country, Bangladesh. IT is one of the most important division required for a country to prosper. And the government has been continuosly making progress in the IT division. And one of the biggest target of our country is the " Vision '21 " The government has set a target to digitalize Bangladesh by 2021. Therefore, we bring the easiest method of digitalizing our education system. This method will not only help our country to become digitalized but it will also save a lot of money and effort along with those this method will decrease the use of paper at an outstanding percentage! Thus our ecological status will become better and our nature pollution will be decreased.
